Acknowledgments

These templates are built on WorkAdventure, an amazing open-source platform created by TheCodingMachine. We extend our heartfelt thanks to their fantastic team for their exceptional support and dedication to the community.

Most of these maps were originally created for the CoCreation.World community project, an ambitious collaborative initiative that brought together creators and developers. While production activities were placed on hold in 2025, this project represents a testament to the potential of collective creativity within the WorkAdventure ecosystem.

Special thanks to withJulio for their exceptional contributions in designing stunning assets and creating immersive worlds that brought these templates to life.
